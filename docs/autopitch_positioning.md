# AutoPitch Positioning: Architectural BD Control Plane

## 1. The Problem
Architectural principals spend 20-40 hours per month manually drafting proposals. Generic AI (ChatGPT) lacks AEC context, halluncinates fee schedules, and doesn't understand specific project constraints (GFA, construction cost, phase-based delivery).

## 2. The Solution: AutoPitch
AutoPitch is not a "text generator"—it is an **Architectural Business Development Control Plane**. It converts raw project briefs and firm profiles into structured, winning proposals grounded in AEC industry standards.

## 3. Key Differentiators
- **Deterministic Fee Logic:** Automatically calculates standard 6-12% fee ranges split by industry-standard phases (SD, DD, CD, Bidding, CA).
- **Firm Profile Grounding:** Injects specific firm specialties, licenses, and notable projects into every section.
- **Structured Output:** Delivers high-signal JSON that can be piped directly into invoicing or project management tools (like PapaBase).
- **A-MEM Integrated:** Recalls prior project wins and "preferred language" to maintain a consistent brand voice.

## 4. Sellable Hooks
- **"Pitch in 5 Minutes, Not 5 Hours":** Go from a 1-paragraph project brief to a 7-section structured proposal instantly.
- **"Precision Estimation":** Use your firm's historical data to ground fee schedules in reality, not guesswork.
- **"Brand Consistency":** Ensure every principal in the firm writes with the same winning style.

## 5. Ideal Customer Profile (AEC)
- **Small-to-Midsize Firms (5-50 staff):** Where principals are still the primary BD drivers.
- **Solo Practitioners:** Who need to look "enterprise-ready" without an administrative assistant.
- **Commercial & Institutional Specialists:** Where structured proposals and fee transparency are mandatory.

## 6. Pilot Strategy
1.  **Ingest 3 Past Wins:** Load successful past proposals into A-MEM to "tune" the voice.
2.  **Run 1 Live Brief:** Generate a proposal for an active lead.
3.  **Validate JSON Output:** Export to PDF/Markdown for client delivery.
